# Style prompt patterns

## Basic formula

```text
<language / scene>, <genre>, <mood>, <vocal type>, <key instruments>, <production feel>, <hook quality>
```

## Examples

### Mandopop ballad

```text
Mandopop ballad, emotional male vocal, piano and strings, slow build, heartfelt lyrics, soaring chorus, polished studio production
```

### Youth anthem

```text
Mandopop youth anthem, bright mixed vocals, energetic drums, sparkling guitars, uplifting and nostalgic, big singalong chorus, festival-ready production
```

### Indie folk

```text
Mandarin indie folk, intimate female vocal, acoustic guitar, soft percussion, warm and reflective, natural room feel, gentle emotional lift
```

### Electronic pop

```text
Modern Mandarin electro pop, airy female vocal, synth layers, punchy drums, dreamy but catchy, glossy production, memorable hook
```

## Useful adjectives

### Mood
- bittersweet
- uplifting
- melancholic
- dreamy
- nostalgic
- rebellious
- tender
- cinematic

### Vocal character
- airy
- husky
- youthful
- intimate
- emotional
- powerful
- fragile
- chant-like

### Production feel
- polished studio production
- raw live-band energy
- cinematic build
- stripped-back intro
- festival-sized chorus
- lo-fi texture
- warm analog feel

## Prompt writing tips

- Keep it specific but compact.
- Usually 1 line is enough.
- Do not stuff every idea into one prompt.
- If lyrics already carry the story, let the style prompt focus on sound and emotion.
